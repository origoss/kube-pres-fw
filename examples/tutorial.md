# Slide Ship Tutorial

## Learn the Basics

Let's walk through the controls!

---

# Movement

## Arrow Keys to Fly

- Up/Down: Vertical movement
- Left/Right: Horizontal movement
- The ship has inertia - it drifts!

![crystal](/images/keys.png){x=1000 y=400 w=180 h=120}

---

# Shooting

## Press SPACE to Fire

- Lasers travel in the direction you're facing
- Shoot crystals to reveal images
- Shoot text to highlight it

![crystal](/images/laser.png){x=900 y=400 w=200 h=100}

---

# Navigation

## Moving Between Slides

- Fly to the RIGHT edge → Next slide
- Fly to the LEFT edge → Previous slide
- Watch the smooth transition!

![crystal](/images/arrows.png){x=1000 y=400 w=150 h=100}

---

# Crystals

## Interactive Image Containers

Crystals contain hidden images:

1. Shoot or bump the crystal
2. Watch it shatter!
3. Image is revealed
4. Shoot the X button to reform

![crystal](/images/crystal-demo.png){x=900 y=400 w=180 h=180}

---

# Text Interaction

## Highlight Important Points

- Fly into text to make it flash
- Perfect for emphasizing key points
- Visual and audio feedback
